General Guidelines

›Warm up 5–10 min before each session (light cardio + dynamic stretches)

›Increase weight when you hit the top of the rep range for 2 sets in a row

›Rest days: active recovery, walking, or light stretching — avoid full rest

›Sleep 7–9 hrs and hit your protein target (0.7–1g per lb of bodyweight)
